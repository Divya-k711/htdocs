For those who performs well in quants all these jobs are the best suitable for them , for their overall development.


1)Data Analyst: Data jobs are ideal jobs for freshers who have strong quant skills.

2)Investment Analyst: It is no surprise, but a lot of finance-related jobs are suitable for students who are strong in quants.

3)Market Researcher: For those students seeking fresher jobs in marketing and sales, but would also like to use their quant skills, a market researcher job is perfect for you.

4)Quant Coach: If corporate jobs for freshers are not your cup of tea and you want to work with quants then an excellent option for you is to teach others! You can opt for a traditional route of a math teacher.



For those who performs well in Logical all these jobs are the best suitable for them , for their overall development.


1)Criminologist: A criminologist is expected to possess excellent observational skills and strong logical reasoning.

2)Marketer analyst: A fresher with strong analytical skills and logical reasoning ability can crunch researched data into meaningful insights that derive course of action of marketing the product.

3)Banker: People trust banks with their money, jewelry and investments. Do you know why? This is because some of the country’s best minds work at banks.

4)Computer Programming: You know that fancy new iPhone or the battery based car – all of it is made out of computer codes. Computer programming partly involves relating numbers and logic to make computers work as per human will.
